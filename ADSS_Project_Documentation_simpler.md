## ğŸ“š Simple Explanations of Terms

### 1. "Heat Units" (GDD)
*   **What is it?**: Think of plants like solar batteries. They need to collect a certain amount of "heat energy" to finish growing.
*   **Why care?**: Counting days on a calendar is not accurate. Counting "Heat Units" is accurate. It helps you harvest at the perfect time.

### 2. Air Wetness (Humidity)
*   **High Wetness**: If the air is very wet (>80%), fungus and diseases can attack your plants easily. Watch out for spots on leaves!
*   **Low Wetness**: If the air is very dry (<40%), plants lose water fast and get thirsty.

### 3. "Rolling Rainfall"
*   **What is it?**: The total amount of rain that fell in the last 7 days combined.
*   **Why care?**: It tells you the true condition of your soil. One heavy rain is okay, but heavy rain for 7 days is a flood risk.

### 4. Wind Speed
*   **Spraying**: Do **NOT** spray medicine if the wind is blowing hard (> 5 m/s). It will blow the medicine away and waste your money.
*   **Storms**: Very strong wind (> 10 m/s) can break tall corn stalks. Be prepared.
